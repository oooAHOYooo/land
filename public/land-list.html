Financing Guide

$15K Down Payment Strategy

Quick Reality Check

Raw land loans usually want 20–50% down, shorter terms (10–15 yrs), and higher rates than home mortgages.

With $15k down, target lot price ≤ $50–75k (20–30% down payment).

"Build soon" lots (septic + driveway + power feasible within ~24 months) get better rates.

Best Financing Paths (Ranked)

1. Local Credit Union "Lot Loan"
10–15 yr, 20–30% down, decent rates. Good for $50–75k parcels with road access.
2. Seller Financing (Owner-Carry)
15 yr amortization, 5-year balloon. Great for odd parcels, low closing costs.
3. Construction-to-Perm (Later)
Buy land now, refi into 30-yr mortgage when ready to build. Requires real plans.

Monthly Payment Examples

$60k lot, $15k down, $45k financed $511-595/mo
$50k lot, $15k down, $35k financed $463/mo
Rule of thumb: Keep monthly $450–$650 by targeting $45–$65k financed on 10–15 yr terms.

Hidden Costs to Budget

Survey & stakes $1.5–4k
Perc test & soil engineer $800–3k
Driveway (gravel, 200–400 ft) $6–20k
Power drop (future) $3–15k
Well & septic (future) $8–15k each
